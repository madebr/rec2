# Known bugs in Carmageddon II

- Pressint 't'(=change target lock) segfaults the game:
  - in pratcam mode
  - during missions (e.g. boulder bash)
- Sometimes when using Cheat Mode's No Damage No Crushage No Wastage, you can still get damaged in rare occasions.
  - Specific case that lead to this addition was on Shove Thy Neighbor playing Purple Piledriver, hit a streetlight while under the effects of Solid Granite Car and somehow damaged the car.
- Enabling music on the options menu doesn't work.
- Sometimes using the SMARTBASTARD keyboard cheat to instantly complete a race/mission can cause a crash to desktop.
- The Slaughter Mortar powerup, when used repeatedly on an opponent, can very rarely cause the game to crash to desktop.
  - Had two Cop Cars cornered on Shove Thy Neighbor (custom RACES.TXT setup), fired about 5 or 6 Slaughter Mortars at them both, game crashed to desktop as soon as the explosion happened.
- Sometimes cheat keybinds stop working.
  - This needs additional testing, the incident in question was where CTRL + SHIFT keybinds (like Opponent Repulsificator) stopped working entirely after a while, while other keybinds like SHIFT + ALT and normal numpad keybinds all continued to work.
